After closing the doors to 'Ate-Bit' back in 1983 due to questionable health and saftey practices, the our team worked tirelessly to open those doors once again. In 1996 we celebrated our success in accomplishing that goal. 8-Bite was a restaurant empire. In more modern days though, numbers have diminished. As appreciation toward 2D games has decreased we've gradually made the impossible shift from a business that's sole purpose was to bank on a trend into a family-run business in it the game only for a passion toward the industry. 8-Bite may not be what it once was, but now it means something to us.
